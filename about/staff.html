Our Staff

Officers


James R. McCabe

President & Principal Officer

James McCabe has been a Member in Good Standing of Local 500 for approximately 30 years. He started in 1984 with Buono Beef Co., which, at the time, was a nonunion company. With help from Bill O'Farrell Jr., the Shop Steward at Twin Brothers, and the Union, they were successful in getting a contract at Buono Beef Co.

In the early 90s, he was put on the Executive Board by Bill O'Farrell, where he served approximately 3.5 years and was involved with several organizing drives including Quaker Valley Foods and Miami Aircraft support team. He also became the Business Agent for Quaker Valley Foods and was an overlapping Business Agent at Septa alongside Joe Coughlin.

Besides working at Buono Beef Co., he also worked at Acme Markets for nearly 25 years, serving as Shop Steward at both Buono Beef Co. and Acme Markets.

Franklin J. Gallagher

Recording Secretary

Frank Gallagher has worked at Acme for the last 2 years, but has previously worked for Sysco foods for 25 years, 6 or 7 of which he spent serving as a Steward. He has been a Teamster since 1989, which means he has served for about 26 years.
